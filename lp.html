What is a GPU?

A GPU, or Graphics Processing Unit, is a specialized processor designed to handle complex calculations required for rendering high-quality visuals and graphics.

Modern GPUs are at the core of gaming, video editing, 3D modeling, and animation, delivering lifelike graphics and seamless performance.

Whether you are a gamer, designer, researcher, or content creator, a powerful GPU unlocks new levels of speed, efficiency, and visual quality.
